Oil Expeller

These Oil Expellers are specially designed to obtain the highest possible level of oil from the seeds. Its Chamber is Fitted with the worms which is made of most durable material. Whole body is made of pure Iron included the Bearings, Gears and Axcel Shaft.

It can Extract Oil from

  • Mustard
  • Sunflower
  • Coconut
  • Sesame

Specifications

Production Capacity: 80 kg/hour - 100 kg/hour
Weight: 480kg
Color: Metallic Green

Requirements

Electricity Connection: Three Phase Connection is must.
Power: 15 HP is best for this machine. However can also work on 12 HP.
